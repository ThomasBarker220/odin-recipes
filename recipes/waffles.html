Waffles

Description

This simple recipe will have you cooking crisp, fluffy waffles in no time. With few ingredients and cooking equipment needed, these waffles are a great addition to any breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
